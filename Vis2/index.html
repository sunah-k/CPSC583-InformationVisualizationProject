SONG POPULARITY TRAITS

This visualization shows data on the popularity of the songs and their specific traits from Spotify.
The goal is to find corelations between song characteristics and popularity
The traits shown here are danceability, energy, acousticness, and valence.
Each bar is a song, which are composed of 4 main traits
The more right angle the song is, the more popular it is

Danceability: How suitable a track is for dancing
Energy: Perceptual measure of intensity and activity, eg. more energy tends to feel faster, louder, and noisy
Acousticness: The confidence measure of whether a track is acoustic
Valence: Musical positiveness of a track, eg. happy vs. sad sounding
